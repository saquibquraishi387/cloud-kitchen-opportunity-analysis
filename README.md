# ☁️ Cloud Kitchen Opportunity Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Analyzed 50,000 real food orders across India to identify 
the most profitable locations to open delivery-only cloud 
kitchens — and built a complete data-backed business strategy 
with location-specific pricing, menu and timing recommendations.

## 💡 The Core Idea
People near colleges, hospitals, railway stations and IT parks 
have limited food options and pay high delivery costs from 
far restaurants. A cloud kitchen within 1-2km solves both 
problems — better food at lower delivery cost.

## 📊 Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle — Food Ordering Behaviour Dataset
- **Size:** 50,000 orders | 19 columns
- **Fields:** City, Cuisine, Order Time, Age, Mood,
  Hunger Level, Discount, Delivery Fee, Rating, Weather

## ❓ Business Questions Answered
1. Which city has highest order volume and spending?
2. What time of day has most orders per city?
3. Which cuisine should our cloud kitchen serve?
4. Do discounts actually increase customer loyalty?
5. Which restaurant type gives best rating and repeat rate?
6. BONUS: Which mood/hunger combo leads to highest spend?
7. BONUS: Does rain increase orders and change cuisine preference?

## 🎯 Target Locations & Pricing Strategy

| Location | Price Range | Peak Hours | Menu Focus |
|----------|------------|------------|------------|
| 💻 IT Parks | ₹250-400 | 1pm-3pm, 9pm-12am | Premium, Variety |
| 🏥 Hospitals | ₹80-150 | All day | Healthy, Combos |
| 🏫 Colleges | ₹80-180 | 9pm-2am (surge) | Biryani, Fast Food |
| 🚂 Stations | ₹100-200 | 6am-11pm | Quick, Grab & Go |

## 🔑 Key Findings

### 💰 Discount Strategy
- Discounts have **ZERO impact** on loyalty
- Repeat rate with discount: 49.96%
- Repeat rate without discount: 49.98%
- **Invest in quality — not discounts**

### 👥 Customer Segments
- Age **36-45 spends most** (₹551) with best loyalty (50.78%)
- **IT Park workers** are the most valuable customers
- Students spend least (₹544) but order highest volume

### ⏰ Timing Insights
- Orders are **equal 24/7** — cloud kitchen can operate round the clock
- **Weekday spending higher** (₹549 vs ₹546 weekend)
- IT Parks and colleges are weekday heavy — perfect alignment

### 🍽️ Menu Insights
- **Desserts** is most ordered cuisine (8,452 orders)
- **Fast Food** has highest repeat rate (50.57%)
- **South Indian** has highest avg spend (₹552)

### 🧠 Behavioural Insights
- **Lazy + Low hunger** customers spend most (₹553)
- Send push notifications at **8-10pm** — not meal times
- Rain shifts preference to **Fast Food** — auto-trigger promos

## ✅ Top Recommendations
1. Open IT Park unit in **Bangalore first** — highest spending power
2. Never discount — **repeat rate is identical** with or without discount
3. Core menu: **Desserts + Fast Food + South Indian**
4. Operate **24/7** — demand exists at all hours
5. Push notifications at **8-10pm** targeting lazy customers
6. Auto-trigger **Fast Food promos** on rainy days
7. Customize **price and menu per location type
